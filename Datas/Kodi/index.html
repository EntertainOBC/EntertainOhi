Android Phones

Open Kodi On Your Android Device, On The Top Left Bar You Should See A Settings Icon Press On It, Then Press On System Settings, Goto Add-ons, Press On Unknown Sources And Press Yes, Go To Home Screen With The Back Button On Your Phone, Go Back To The Settings Icon, Then You Should See File Manager Press On It, Then Press On Add Source, Press On None Then Type In http://fusion.tvaddons.ag Then Press Okay, Then Press Under Where It Saids Enter A Name For This Media Source Then Type In Fusion, Go Back To The Home Screen With The Back Buttton On Your Phone, Then On The Left Hand Side You Should See Add-ons Press On It, On The Top Left Bar You Should See A Box Icon Press On It, Press On Install From Zip, Then Press On Fusion, Then Press On Kodi-Repos, Then Press On English, Then Scroll Down And Find Repository.Exodus-1.0.1.zip The 1.0.1 Might Chang Because That Is The Version, Then Press On Install From Respoitory, And Find Exodus Repository, And Press On Video-Add-Ons, And Press On Exodus And Press On Install, And Go Back To The Home Screen With Back Button On Your Phone. The Left Hand Side You Should See Add-Ons Press On It, Then On The Right You Should See Exodus Press On It And Your Done!.

Android TV Boxes

Open Kodi, Go To System Underneath It You Should See File Manager Click on It, Then Click On Add Source, Click On None, Type In http://fusion.tvaddons.ag Then Press Done, Click Below Where It Said Enter A Name For This Media Source, Then Type In Fusion Then Press Done, Go Back To The Home Screen, Go To System And Click On It, Go To Add-Ons, Click On Install From Zip File, Then Click On Fusion, Then Click On Kodi-Repos, Then Click On English, And Find Respoitory.Exodus-1.0.1 The 1.0.1 Might Chang Because That Is The Verison Click On It, And Click On Install From Repository, Find Exodus Respoitory And Click On It, And Click On Video-Add-Ons, And Click On Exodus, And Click On Install. And Go Back To The Home Screen, And Click On System, Then Click On Add-Ons, And My Add-Ons, And Click On All, Find Exodus And Click On It, And Click On Open And Your Done!.
